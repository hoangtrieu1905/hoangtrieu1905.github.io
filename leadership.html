  • MATH-090: College Algebra (Fall 2024, Fall 2025)
  • MATH-220: Integration Methods (Spring 2026)

  • MATH-320: Differential Equation (Spring 2026)

Statistics for College Algebra (Fall 2024):

  • SI participants scored an average of 126 points on homework compared to 106 points for non-participants, and 89% on the midterm compared to 78.9% for non-participants.

Statistic for Multivariate Calculus (Fall 2025):

  • Achieved 100% pass rate (0% DFW) for 19/60 attendees compared to the 12.2% DFW rate of the non-attendee group.
  • Driven statistically improvement (p=0.04) in student performance, generating a 5.8% lift in quiz scores while non-attendees declined by 1.8%.
  • Boosted at-risk student outcomes by 15.6 % points on final exams (74% vs. 58% for the at-risk, non-attendee group)
  • (Midterm report): On average, we record a net 14% increase in scores of all SI participants from Quiz 3 (no SI) to Quiz 4 (with SI help). The most significant % points increase was 69.2%.
